James Real Estate Services, Inc. (JRES) is a full-service commercial real estate appraisal and advisory firm based in Denver, Colorado. Our experienced team offers a wide range of real estate appraisal services and products that help our clients effectively analyze commercial real estate assets, residential real estate assets and real estate markets - as well as manage asset risk and realize asset value in a constantly changing economic environment and real estate marketplace.
